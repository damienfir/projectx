Always get the best of your photo collection.

When returning from a vacation or an event, you probably have a clutter of photos on your computer or in your smartphone, waiting for you to organize them manually.

We leverage the advances in image processing and machine learning to automatically select the best of your photos with zero effort from your side.

Our technology is based on recognizing the important features in photos such as colorfulness, blurriness, amount of details, presence of faces and smiles. Our solution picks the best of several similar photos according to these objectives criteria to make sure your composition will be representative of your whole photo collection.

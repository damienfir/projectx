About

We consider ourselves curators of your pictures. Our mission is to help you manage your growing collection of photos and enjoy them in ways that you have not experienced before.

For instance, we make beautiful picture summaries for you automatically. Done manually, this takes hours or even days of effort. We use our expertise in the field of computer vision, machine learning, image processing, and artificial intelligence to create algorithms that understand the content of your pictures and create aesthetically pleasing presentations in just a few seconds.

Creating a poster

You may either upload a set of pictures from your computer or you may import your pictures from Dropbox, Facebook and other cloud providers. After the upload is over, we create the summary and display it on your screen.

Sharing

You can click on ‘Share’ to download the poster in full resolution for emailing or printing, or share it directly on Facebook, Google+, and more.

Automatic picture management

People spend a great deal of time organizing their photos after a trip or some such event. We make sure you never have to go through the painstaking process of organizing your photos again. We choose the best of them, and automatically create eye-catching summaries in the form of posters and albums. You can print them, email them, or share them on social media. You can also let us print your posters and albums for you so you may mount them on your walls or place them on your coffee tables, and cherish them, over and over.

FAQ & Terms

Who owns the copyright to my photos?

You do. By uploading the pictures you only give us the right to process them and store the processed summaries so that we may be able to provide them to you in the future. You also give us the right to pass them to third party service who may print them for you in some form.

Sometimes my images are cropped incorrectly. Why?

Our algorithms can make mistakes. It is not our intention. We are constantly working to improve them.

Why does the upload take so much time?

The cause is likely to be be one or more of the following:
  • The internet connection is slow
  • You are uploading many pictures (more than a hundred)
  • The photos are very large (greater than 5 MB each)

API

We provide APIs for our following core technologies:

Cropping

Automatic image cropping and resizing to fit any aspect ratio and any required resolution.

Dominant colors

Compute dominant colors in an image

Image selection

Choosing the best subset of images from several images.

Image attractiveness

Image attractiveness score computation.

Enhancement

Automatic image correction and enhancement.

Face detection

Given a photo, our API detects if one or more faces are present, as well as their location in the image and size.
We also provide the complete package of automatic summary creating to be run on standalone photobooths, printing kiosks, and photo printing websites.
